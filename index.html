WELCOME!

Welcome to Ben Meskerem Prime Hospital and College. Our institution is dedicated to delivering exceptional standards of medical care to all individuals seeking our services. Within our esteemed College, we offer a comprehensive range of educational programs, including Bachelor's degrees in Nursing, Pharmacy, and Laboratory Technician fields.

Our commitment is to provide the utmost level of medical excellence and academic quality, ensuring the holistic development of our students and the optimal health and well-being of our patients.

Our
Health Departments

Department of Internal Medicine

Offers 24/7 inpatient and outpatient services in areas including infectious diseases, cardiovascular, nephrology, endocrinology, rheumatology, gastroenterology, pulmonology, and more. We provide comprehensive diagnostic support with lab tests, ECG, ultrasonography, and radiology.

Department of Gynecology and Obstetrics

Our OB/GYN department, led by specialists, midwives, and nurses, provides comprehensive services in obstetrics, gynecology, family planning, fertility management, and MTCT services through collaboration with the Regional Health Bureau.

Department of Pediatrics

The Pediatrics Department provides 24/7 pediatric and neonatal care, integrating seamlessly with hospital diagnostics. With a state-of-the-art inventory, including nebulizers and incubators, it ensures top-tier care of the higher standards for our young patients.

Department of Dentistry

Our state-of-the-art facility, equipped with skilled dental professionals and advanced tri-nitron equipment, provides comprehensive dental services, including scaling, fillings, extractions, root canals, and permanent/removable prosthetics like jacket crowns.

Department Of Dermatology And Venereology

Specializing in comprehensive medical care, including cutting-edge cryosurgery and venerology services, our department collaborates seamlessly with others in the hospital. This ensures holistic patient care and well-rounded medical support.

Department of Diagnostic Radiology

Our radiography department specializes in comprehensive diagnostic services, including Hysterosalpingography, Cystourethrography, Intravenous Pyelography, and ultrasonography, ensuring high professionalism and expertise in medical imaging.

Department of Surgery

Under an experienced Plastic Surgeon, our Department provides 24/7 outpatient and inpatient services, including emergencies. We cover diverse surgical procedures, including General and specific, Plastic and Reconstructive, and Pediatric Surgery.

Emergency Department

A critical healthcare facility, meticulously designed to offer immediate medical care to individuals facing urgent or life-threatening conditions, operates around the clock, ensuring swift and proficient medical attention for those in need..
